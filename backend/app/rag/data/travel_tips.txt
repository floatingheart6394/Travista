TRAVEL TIPS AND BEST PRACTICES

General Travel Tips:
1. Book flights 6-8 weeks in advance for better prices
2. Use price comparison websites like Kayak, Skyscanner, or Google Flights
3. Avoid traveling during peak seasons for better deals
4. Pack light - most airlines allow 7kg for carry-on
5. Get travel insurance for international trips
6. Check visa requirements 3 months before travel
7. Inform your bank about travel dates to avoid card blocks
8. Download offline maps before traveling
9. Keep copies of important documents in cloud storage
10. Learn basic phrases in the local language

Packing Essentials:
- Passport and travel documents
- Travel adapter and power banks
- Medications and first aid kit
- Comfortable walking shoes
- Lightweight clothes suitable for climate
- Toiletries and sunscreen
- Reusable water bottle
- Phone charger and cables

Budget Travel Tips:
- Stay in hostels or use Airbnb for cheaper accommodation
- Use public transportation instead of taxis
- Eat at local restaurants instead of tourist spots
- Book accommodation with kitchen facilities to cook meals
- Use free walking tours to explore cities
- Visit museums on free entry days
- Use travel apps like Rome2Rio for transport planning

Safety Tips:
- Keep valuables in hotel safe
- Use official taxis or ride-sharing apps
- Avoid displaying expensive items
- Travel with copies of documents
- Keep emergency contacts handy
- Register with your embassy if traveling to high-risk areas
- Blend in with local dress code
- Stay aware of surroundings in crowded areas
