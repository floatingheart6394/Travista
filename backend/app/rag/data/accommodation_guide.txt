ACCOMMODATION GUIDE FOR TRAVELERS

Hotel Booking Tips:
- Book directly with hotels for potential upgrades and perks
- Check reviews on TripAdvisor, Google Reviews, and Booking.com
- Compare prices across Booking.com, Agoda, Hotels.com, and Expedia
- Book 2-3 months ahead for peak season
- Look for hidden fees (resort fees, city taxes)
- Check cancellation policies carefully
- Star ratings: 3-star good for budget, 4-star comfortable, 5-star luxury
- Negotiate directly if booking last-minute

Airbnb Tips:
- Look for super-hosts with high ratings
- Check for entire apartment vs shared space
- Read recent reviews carefully
- Confirm cleaning fee and additional costs
- Book accommodations with kitchen to save on food
- Consider location near public transport
- First-time discounts often available (typically $20-40 off)

Hostels:
- Great for solo travelers and backpackers
- Average cost $15-30 per night in most countries
- Private rooms available if you prefer privacy
- Often includes breakfast and social activities
- Best platforms: Hostelworld, Booking.com, HostelBookers
- Check reviews for cleanliness and safety

Budget Accommodation (<$50/night):
- Hostels: $15-30
- Budget hotels: $20-50
- Airbnb basics: $25-45
- Guesthouses: $15-40

Mid-Range Accommodation ($50-150/night):
- 3-star hotels: $60-120
- Nice Airbnbs: $50-130
- Resorts: $70-150
- Boutique hotels: $80-140

Luxury Accommodation (>$150/night):
- 4-5 star hotels: $150-500+
- Resorts: $150-400+
- Luxury Airbnbs: $150-500+
- Private villas: $200-1000+

Things to Check:
1. Location - walk-ability to attractions and transport
2. WiFi quality - important for remote workers
3. Bathroom - private or shared?
4. Air conditioning/heating - crucial in extreme climates
5. 24-hour reception - for emergencies
6. Safe deposit boxes - for valuables
7. Breakfast included - affects overall budget
8. Cancellation policy - flexibility is valuable

Airport Accommodation:
- Stay near airport if arriving late
- Transit hotels offer cheap 6-12 hour stays
- Capsule hotels in Asia are affordable options
- Book in advance for popular airports

Booking Strategy:
- Off-season = better prices and fewer tourists
- Mid-week (Tue-Thu) is usually cheaper than weekends
- Loyalty programs often have hidden discounts
- Credit cards may offer travel protections
- Last-minute bookings (within 48 hours) can have deals
- Weekly/monthly bookings often have discounts
