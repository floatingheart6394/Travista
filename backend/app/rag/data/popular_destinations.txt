POPULAR TRAVEL DESTINATIONS GUIDE

Europe:
Paris, France - "The City of Light" offers the Eiffel Tower, museums, and world-class cuisine. Best time to visit: April-June or September-October.

Barcelona, Spain - Known for Gaud√≠'s architecture, beaches, and vibrant nightlife. Budget-friendly with great food scene.

Rome, Italy - Ancient history with Colosseum, Vatican City, and Roman Forum. Best visited in spring or fall.

Amsterdam, Netherlands - Canals, bicycles, museums, and laid-back atmosphere. Great for budget travelers.

London, UK - Royal palaces, historical landmarks, and diverse culture. Museums and galleries are mostly free.

Asia:
Bangkok, Thailand - Affordable city with temples, markets, and delicious street food. Very budget-friendly.

Tokyo, Japan - Modern technology meets traditional culture. Excellent public transport and safe streets.

Bali, Indonesia - Beautiful beaches, temples, and rice paddies. Excellent value for money.

Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam - Affordable food, vibrant energy, and historical sites. Great for backpackers.

Singapore - Modern city-state with excellent food and shopping. More expensive but worth it.

India:
Kerala - "God's Own Country" with backwaters, beaches, and hill stations. Visit Kollam for Ashtamudi Lake backwaters, Thangassery beach, and historic lighthouse.

Goa - Beautiful beaches, Portuguese architecture, and vibrant nightlife. Budget-friendly beach destination.

Jaipur - "Pink City" with majestic palaces, forts, and colorful markets. Part of the Golden Triangle circuit.

Mumbai - India's financial capital with colonial architecture, Bollywood, and street food culture.

Delhi - Historic capital with Red Fort, India Gate, and diverse cuisine. Gateway to North India.

Agra - Home to the Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Varanasi - Spiritual city on Ganges River with ancient temples and evening aartis.

Kollam (Quilon), Kerala - Historic port city famous for Ashtamudi Lake backwaters, cashew trading, Thangassery lighthouse, Palaruvi waterfalls, and traditional houseboat cruises. Best time: October-March.

Americas:
New York City, USA - The City That Never Sleeps. High prices but endless entertainment.

Mexico City, Mexico - Rich culture, museums, and amazing food. Affordable compared to North America.

Lima, Peru - Gateway to Machu Picchu with excellent restaurants and museums.

Rio de Janeiro, Brazil - Beaches, Christ the Redeemer statue, and vibrant culture.

Vancouver, Canada - Mountains, ocean, and modern city. Good for outdoor activities.

Africa:
Cairo, Egypt - Pyramids, Nile River, and ancient history. Most iconic destination in Africa.

Cape Town, South Africa - Stunning beaches, Table Mountain, and wine country.

Marrakech, Morocco - Medinas, souks, and Atlas Mountains. Affordable and exotic.

Tanzania - Safari experiences in Serengeti and Kilimanjaro climbing.

Oceania:
Sydney, Australia - Beautiful beaches, Opera House, and laid-back vibe.

Melbourne, Australia - Coffee culture, street art, and vibrant nightlife.

Auckland, New Zealand - Adventure capital with hiking, bungy jumping, and stunning nature.

Fiji - Tropical paradise with beaches and island culture.
